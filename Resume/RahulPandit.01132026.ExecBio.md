## **Executive Bio**

Rahul Pandit is an engineering executive with over 15 years of experience building and scaling AI-driven platforms across Big Tech and high-growth startups. He has led global engineering organizations, owned P&L responsibility, and delivered products that operate at national and global scale.

Rahul has taken multiple businesses from zero to market, including AI-first platforms and large consumer systems. His work spans ML-driven product development, platform modernization, and operational scaling under real revenue and cost constraints. At Visible Wireless, he tripled revenue while reducing operating costs and led the transition to a cloud-native 5G architecture. At Amazon, he launched new businesses and ML-powered Alexa experiences used by millions of customers.

He is known for pairing technical depth with strong operating discipline, building leadership teams, and aligning engineering execution tightly to business outcomes. Rahul has worked closely with founders, CEOs, and boards to translate strategy into durable platforms and predictable delivery.
